Key Features

Target market identified

Travellers now have a true alternative for them. Nudly has almost no competitors in this space. We want an unfair share of the $7.6 trillion US (and rising) market.

Businesses get paid fast

All smart contracts are validated within our own ecosystem. This makes for superfast and efficient verification. Payments are handled by local escrow agents for prompt payment.

Regulator friendly

Our in-country escrow agents are fully licensed and compliant with local regulatory authorities to cover micro-transactions for restaurants, souvenirs, tours and sightseeing.



How Nudly Works

Download the app, signup and link to your bank. Now travellers can load their account any time, even whilst overseas. They can also convert to local currency (at mid-market rates) whenever they like.

Simple To Use

Scan the business's QR code, enter amount and send. Business and customer are notified by SMS. Businesses can also connect their POS via our APIs for seamless payments.

Safe And Secure

Nudly will have in-country escrow payments in 90 countries within 2 years. They provide the first of four levels of verification for our smart contracts. This gives us fast and efficient processing, and prompt payment.

No More High Fees

Using overseas ATMs always incur high fees. And then they clip you with the exchange rate. And not all cards are accepted overseas, so you have to carry multiple cards, increasing your risk.

Regulator Friendly

Our in-country escrow agents are fully compliant with local regulatory authorities. This provides the transparency required whilst blockchain protects their privacy.

What is Nudly?

Nudly is a blockchain payments platform for the travel money industry. It gives travellers an alternative way to pay for accommodation, transportation, entertainment and attractions.

What cryptocurrencies can I use to purchase?

We accept Ether and Bitcoin

How can I participate in the ICO Token sale?

You need to register here. Then you can login to the portal and make your purchase.

How do I benefit from the ICO Token?

Buying early gives you 10X, 5X and Referral bonuses which gives you extra NUDX tokens that you can trade on exchanges. You can also convert NUDX into cash and use it as travel money.

Why do I have to register to participate?

Many countries adhere to international KYC (Know Your Customer) protocols to make sure participants are bona fide and not money laundering for terrorist or criminal organisations.

Whhat's the soft cap?

The soft cap is $50,000 US. This allows us to complete the platform and produce an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) to take to market. After this, ongoing development is required as we evolve.

What will happen if soft cap is not reached?

If we don't hit our soft cap by 30 Nov 2019, all funds will be returned and we will continue to bootstrap while we engage traditional investment sources.

How do I get the most coins cheaply?

Participate in the 10X Round. This gives you 10 coins for every one bought. Each NUDX token is $1US and minimum purchase is $100 - so that's 1000 coins. Plus you get 100 everytime you refer someone who also participates. They have to buy for you to get the Referral bonus.

When can I trade NUDX?

Once we hit the soft cap, we will move to list NUDX tokens for trading. We email everyone when updates are available.

What if I need to sell my NUDX?

Nudly is not allowed to buy back any coins until after Year 3. In the meantime, your option is to sell them on your preferred exchange.
